# Kontour Opportunity Index

This index tracks projects reviewed through the Kontour AI trust-infrastructure lens, plus internal product opportunities that build on top of the Kontour stack. Detailed entries live in `docs/opportunities/` as the corpus grows.

The 2026-05-29 explorations share a thesis: the durable asset is a set of **derived-trust
primitives** (claims inferred from other claims, freshness-as-state, support-strength,
materiality, temporal claims, multi-party adjudication), captured in
`surface-derived-trust-primitives.md`. The products below kept dying on go-to-market while the
primitives recurred — so several entries are "primitive yes, horizontal product no."
